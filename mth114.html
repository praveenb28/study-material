Welcome to MTH114 - Differential Equations

Explore the theory and applications of differential equations in various fields.

Course Description

This course covers the fundamental concepts of differential equations, including first-order and higher-order equations, systems of equations, and applications.

  • Credits: 6
  • Prerequisites: MTH111 - Calculus I, MTH112 - Calculus II, MTH113 - Linear Algebra

Course Outline

  • Introduction to Differential Equations
  • First-Order Differential Equations
  • Higher-Order Differential Equations
  • Systems of Differential Equations
